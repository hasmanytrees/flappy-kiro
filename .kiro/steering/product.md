# Product Overview

## Flappy Kiro

A browser-based Flappy Bird clone featuring the Kiro logo as the player character. The game demonstrates classic arcade mechanics with modern web technologies and Kiro's brand aesthetic.

### Core Gameplay
- Single-button control (spacebar or click) for jumping
- Navigate through purple pipe obstacles
- Score points by passing through gaps
- Increasing difficulty through continuous pipe spawning

### Visual Identity
- Dark theme with Kiro brand colors (purple accents on dark backgrounds)
- Smooth animations and rotation effects
- Retro-inspired gameplay with modern polish
- Kiro logo as the playable sprite

### Target Audience
Workshop participants learning game development with AI assistance during AWS Re:Invent.
